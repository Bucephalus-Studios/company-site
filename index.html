Welcome to Bucephalus Studios!

A solo indie game development studio from Ohio creating weird games and contributing to free open-source software.

Create your own cult, perform demonic rituals, and amass a following that threatens empires. It's time to play god. CultGame is a retro strategy RPG where you start your own religion in a procedurally generated fantasy world complete with haunting ASCII graphics.
