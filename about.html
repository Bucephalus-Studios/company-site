About Bucephalus Studios


Bucephalus Studios LLC is a software company founded in 2021 by Jeff Stevens. We are dedicated toward making original games that are not plagued by problems like corporatized design, predatory monetization, or pushing political agendas. Games are about fun; let's keep them that way!

The team at Bucephalus Studios consists of a single full-time developer, Jeff Stevens, and a motley crew of freelancers, friends, and family. We're not beholden to publishers or investors, so we're bound to make something absurd. We hope you'll find it fun, too!

We are also an ardent supporter of free and open-source software. Lots of our code, tools, and libraries are available to download and use for FREE on the "Software" tab of this website and on our Github.

About Jeff Stevens

Hi, I'm Jeff Stevens, and this was me as a wee lad.

I'm in my 20s now, and these memories of meeting up with my lunchtable friends in MMORPGs, braving the wild-west of early internet forums and image boards, and playing every flash game I could find on Newgrounds made me the game developer I am today.

I work from home doing business as Bucephalus Studios designing games, writing code, and doing the mish-mash of other tasks that comes with pulling a game into the world kicking and screaming. Outside of work you'll find me going to the gym, reading fantasy novels, or cooking for family and friends.
