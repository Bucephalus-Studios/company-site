About Bucephalus Studios


Bucephalus Studios LLC is a software company founded in 2021 by Jeff Stevens. We are dedicated toward making interesting, original games that are not plagued by problems like corporatized design, predatory monetization, or pushing political agendas. Games are about fun; let's keep them that way!

The team at Bucephalus Studios consists of a single full-time developer, Jeff Stevens, and a motley crew of contractors, freelancers, friends, and family that help out as needed. We are a small, independent studio that values creativity, originality, and fun above all else. We are not beholden to any publisher or investor, and we make games that we want to play. We hope you enjoy them too!

We are also a big supporter of free open-source software. Check out our software page to see our programs and code you can download for free!
